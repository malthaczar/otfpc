About Us

On The Fly Pest Control is a family owned and operated pest control company servicing counties as north as Alachua and as south as Hillsborough. With Robert E. "Butch" Bryant as the CPO we have over 25 years of experience killing and removing bugs from homes all across central Florida!

Services we Offer

We provide residential and commericial pest control with experience you can trust and some of the best prices on the market. If you have roaches, ants, silverfish, wasps, spiders, or any other creepy crawler; we have you covered.

Do you have bats or rats living among you? We can provide an exclusion or removal service to rid you of their unwanted presence. We offer chemical and nonchemical methods to deal with your unique problem.

Bed bugs have unfortunately made a resurgence in Florida, if you are concerned about a bed bug infestation give us a call! We have effective methods to detect, kill, and remove bed bugs.
